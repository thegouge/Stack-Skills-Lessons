Terminology
- Gfall or shift: Gravity shift fast fall
- G-Kick: Gravity Kick
- RG-Kick: Rapid Gravity Kick performed by G-Canceling the G-Kick then activating another in sequence (square->L1->R1->square
repeat)
- DRG-Kick: Delayed RG-Kick same as RG-kick but the kicks are delayed between each one
- G-Cancel: The ending Lag of both Gravity kicks and slide kicks can both be interrupted by deactivating gravity with L1
- Tech landing: You can cancel the crash animation that occurs when falling from a high height by holding a direction and pressing
R2 (mashing R2 is okay) before hitting the ground

All health upgrades are optional and should be used dependant on circumstances. The route is pretty tight on gems until Nushi so try not to spend too much on health so you don’t miss other important upgrades.

When you start a run make sure to close and reopen the application so that the settings are default
Episode 1: From Oblivion (Start 0 gems)
(Upgrades: None)

Awaken with dusty
- Change settings
- Turn off controller speaker for recorded runs
- Set camera and motion sensitivity
- We believe that changing the music and/or sound effect volume can cause the game to randomly crash so try to avoid that
- Skip first part of stairs by walking into trash can
- Run up stairs
- Get to Gravity Tutorial
- Cutscene skip at the end of “Eugieeeeeeeee”
Gravity Tutorial
- Cutscene skip at Kat an arms length away from grabbing Eugie
Episode 2: Shadows Over the City (Start 0 gems)
(Upgrades: Gauge Usage 3 gems [scripted])

Catch Dusty 1
- After gravity cancel hit checkpoint at the stair railing before reaching the end building
Fight Nevi
- Jump Fair into 3 kicks for first two waves
- Left ground nevi drops blue gem which gives you the refill for straggler
- Take care of the ground nevi then the flying to be closer to the straggler
- Quick Kill Straggler by aiming directly at the window where it spawns (second window from the left, middle row of rounded
windows)
Fountain Gem Collection
- Grab first couple gems and after getting one on roof wait for meter to refill
- G-kick to one on far wall, then G-kick to one on ground, G-kick to one on TV
- G-kick to the rest of the gems and land on can
- Upgrade gauge to Lv 2 (3 gems [scripted])
- During auto-save before Ep. 3 grab 10 gem on top of fountain
Episode 3: Home Sweet Home (Start 0 or 10 gems)
(Upgrades: None)

Get Chair
- Long G-Kick the first Are -> L-cancel -> RG-Kick the second Are
- Cut over bridge and in between pipes
Get Desk
- Go over the building and cancel gravity when you fly over it so it can refill by the time you reach the desk
Get Bed
- RG-Kick the Lancar
- Walk over to the barrels and pick one up to throw at the Are to the left, then throw another one at the one behind you
- G-kick the remaining Ares
- RG-Kick the Lancar
